How to Play

  • Click any tile in line with the empty cell to slide it — tiles in between will follow along
  • Use ↑ ↓ ← → to slide a single tile into the empty space
  • Arrange tiles 1 through 15 in order, with the empty cell at the bottom-right
  • The dot on a tile marks it as correctly placed
