This is a simplified 1D model: cars move along a single lane, with a pre-existing dense region (standing-wave jam). Three on-ramps feed traffic. When metering is OFF, ramp inflow keeps the jam alive. When metering is ON, ramp signals throttle inflow based on jam density, allowing the jam to dissipate over time.
