What You’re Seeing

A standing-wave traffic jam can remain long after the original trigger disappears. In this model, heavy mainline demand plus three uncontrolled on-ramps keep the freeway operating right at the edge of capacity. When ramp metering begins, the signals briefly hold some entering traffic on the ramps, preventing fresh surges at the merge points and allowing the mainline to recover.
