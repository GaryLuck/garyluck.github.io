How to Play

  • Slide tiles into the empty space to arrange them in order from 1 to 15.
  • Click or tap any tile adjacent to the empty slot, or click any tile in the same row/column to slide multiple tiles at once.
  • Use Arrow Keys or WASD on keyboard to slide tiles.
  • Switch between Numbers mode and Custom Image modes using the top dropdown.
  • Use the Hint button if you get stuck to see the next optimal step!
